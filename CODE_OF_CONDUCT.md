# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct | 贡献者行为准则

## Our Pledge | 我们的承诺

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

作为成员、贡献者和领导者，我们承诺让每个人在我们的社区中获得无骚扰的参与体验，无论年龄、体型、残疾、种族、性别特征、性别认同和表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教或性取向。

## Our Standards | 我们的标准

**Positive behavior includes | 积极行为包括：**

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness / 展示同理心和善意
- Being respectful of differing opinions / 尊重不同观点
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback / 给予和接受建设性反馈
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing for mistakes / 承担责任并为错误道歉
- Focusing on what is best for the community / 关注对社区最有利的事

**Unacceptable behavior includes | 不可接受的行为包括：**

- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks / 恶意挑衅、侮辱性评论、人身或政治攻击
- Public or private harassment / 公开或私下的骚扰
- Publishing others' private information without permission / 未经许可发布他人私人信息
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate / 其他可合理认为不当的行为

## Enforcement | 执行

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

社区领导者负责明确和执行可接受行为的标准，并对任何不当、威胁性、冒犯性或有害行为采取适当和公正的纠正措施。

## Scope | 范围

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

本行为准则适用于所有社区空间，也适用于个人在公共空间中正式代表社区的场合。

## Reporting | 举报

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders at **superopc@proton.me**.

如发现滥用、骚扰或其他不可接受的行为，请向社区负责人举报：**superopc@proton.me**
